1

Registration

Register for the Biggest Loser Challenge by paying $20 before 10:00 AM on the competition start date. PayPal: roni.thoms@gmail.com. Zelle: (732) 789-5550.

2

Initial weigh-in

Use a digital scale, take a clear picture showing the weight, and submit it between 1:00 AM and 10:00 AM on the start date. Use the same scale for the final weigh-in. Please compete fairly and do not manipulate the starting weight with heavy or weighted clothing.

3

Winner calculation

Winners are determined by the highest percentage of starting body weight lost, not by total pounds alone.

4

Ties

If participants tie for a prize position, the combined prize money for the tied positions will be split equally.

5

Prize payment

Prize money will be transferred by PayPal or Zelle after results are finalized.

6

Competition period

The challenge runs for the dates shown above. Submit the final scale photo by 10:00 PM on the final weigh-in date.

7

Winner announcement

Winners will be announced after the competition ends, based on participants who submitted a valid final weight.

8

Community participation

Share daily exercise activities such as steps, miles run, walking, cycling, or gym time, plus healthy meals, recipes, and meal photos. This helps motivate the whole group.

9

Health responsibility

No one is responsible for any health condition arising directly or indirectly from this competition. Participate according to your own health and fitness condition.
